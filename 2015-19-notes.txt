ArCRnY
Atoms which are preserved in their rules
ThSi

They are made by:
Al => ThRnFAr and similar rules ...Ar
H -> CRn... and other rules which start with C...
Rn and Y made in the middle

So
[i] for positions of a terminal in target: say 5, 10
[j] for positions of a terminals in test:
    - impossible if last j > last i, last but 1 j > last but 1 i etc
